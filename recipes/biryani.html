Chicken Biryani

Description

This is the best chicken biryani you'll ever have

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of chicken thighs
  • 1 bag of basmati rice
  • 1 clove of garlic
  1. Place chicken in the microwvae
  2. Place rice in the oven
  3. Don't use any of the garlic
